Case Study

Transforming Educational Institutions in Bangladesh

Company Overview

  • Founded: 2017
  • Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh
  • Website: Clutch Profile

Business Challenge

Outdated administrative processes faced by inefficiencies in managing student records, attendance, and communication.

Solution

Edumax, an education management system offering:

  • Student information management
  • Attendance tracking
  • Dynamic websites
  • ID card services

Results

  • Implemented in 200+ schools and colleges across Bangladesh
  • Enhanced administrative efficiency and reduced manual workload
  • Improved accuracy in attendance tracking and record-keeping
  • Strengthened institutions' online presence
